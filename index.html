What Is It?

Scouthike is an action pack hike that you and your patrol get to plan, along the way is tons of activities relating the 6 SPICES that are: Social, Physical, Intellectual, Character, Emotional and Spiritual. Scouts from all over Victoria are allowed to attend but there is a cap one thound two hundred scouts so first in best dressed.

Where And When Is It

Scouthike will be from the 17th to the 19th of May 2023. It is in Tallarook state forest.
